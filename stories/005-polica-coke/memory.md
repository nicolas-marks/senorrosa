# Working title
Policia & Coke
## What happened

On our return to the United States I had gotten pretty use to getting stoped by the authorities when you crossed state lines. It was almost noon, we had the windows down enjoying the coastal views. Campeche is beutiful. When we entered the town, we were pulled over because of our US plates. I pulled over, rolled down my window and got my travel documents ready. This time was pretty good. I understood mostly what was happening. "What are you doing down here?" Visiting family. "How long are you staying?" Oh we are headed back to the US now! it was a fairly normal interaction until Gina suggested I give him a Coke. Only my pea brain decided to give him 20 pesos to buy a coke. As he handed back my drivers license and was starting to wave me off I plopped 20 pesos in his hand. He looked at me awckwardly, and I looked at him ackwardly. Silence. Gina hissed "Nico" so I dropped him another 20 pesos. Maybe she wanted him to have two cokes. At his point the embarrassment was too much for everyone and the cop said "Ya Vamos" Gina covered he face in embarrasement and off we went.
